Open Positions in Centre for Credible AI

We invite: applications to join our interdisciplinary team advancing reliable and explainable AI.
We offer: flexible appointments from 0.5 FTE to full-time; contracts for 1 or 2 years with the possibility of extension; work in an active team in an excellent scientific environment with access to the state-of-the-art research infrastructure; participation in courses, summer schools, and conferences; competitive salaries (see below, for comparison, the Polish academic full professor salary is ca. 10k PLN gross/month).
How to apply: please submit following documents as pdf files to join@credibleai.eu: motivation letter; research CV; a list of publications / innovations / software solutions; contact details of previous supervisors for reference.
Note: in your motivation letter, specify the teams you wish to work with and consider their specific areas of focus (the RED team is more focused on model analysis methods, BIO on life sciences, BLUE on language models and human interaction, and PHYS on physics and complex systems).
